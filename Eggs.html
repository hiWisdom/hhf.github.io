Healthy Harvester Farms Day Old Broiler Chicken & Layer Chicken

Healthy Harvester Farms Day Old Chicken Stage 1

At stage 1 the chicks are one (1) to two (2) days old and know nothing and are open to learning. At Healthy Harvester Farms our day old stage one chicken are train sensitively of their enviroment. They learn and develope taste for things at Stage one, things such as food, how to eat, what to drink, enviroment preference, relationship e.t.c.

Healthy Harvester Farms Day Old Chicken Stage 2

At stage 2 the chicks are five (5) to fourteen (14) days old. At this stage the chick starts developing personal ability, habit and character. At this point we at Healthy Harvester Farms we maintain strick procedures and chicken growth structure plan which result them setting their daily routeins.

Healthy Harvester Farms Day Old Chicken Stage 3

At stage 3 the chicks are fourteen (14) days to twenty eight (28) days old. The chicks have started producing feathers and can stay without heater from this stage hence forth.
